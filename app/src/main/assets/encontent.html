QuantumDraw's screen (QD from now on)
Connection indicator (colors meaning)
  1. Gray: No connected
  2. Yellow: Connected and waiting for the guest.
  3. Green: Both connected and exchanging draws.
  4. Black: You have selected the eraser.

Coordination tag

The easiest way to agree remotly about who should draw next is by making a little stripe in a corner to warn that you start drawing and erasing it when you pass the turn. The other person does the same using another color

Invite to draw

The funniest feature about QD is that it enables you to share your drawings with another person.

Given that all Android devices are linked to a GMAIL account and that all incoming emails trigger a sound signal, QD is based on GMAIL to function.

So, if you want to invite another person to draw’n chat, you just have to follow the following steps:

  1. Choose "Invite" in your QD menu.
  2. Choose your contact (he must have a GMAIL account synchronized in his phone/tablet).
  3. QD will send your contact an email. Your connection indicator becomes yellow and from there on all you draw in your device will be shared with the other device when it joins the session.
  4. When the other person receives the invitation email, he just needs to click on the link included, choose QD to open it and join the session (your connection indicator becomes green).
  5. You can leave the session whenever you want just by clicking on the "unshare" button at the QD menu (your connection indicator becomes gray).
